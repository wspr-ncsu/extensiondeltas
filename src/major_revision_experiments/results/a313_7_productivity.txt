Synergy Research is a research management solution to save & organize websites, PDFs, and notes; search smartly across all your content; and collaborate efficiently with a team.

The solution is fully integrated with Microsoft Office and helps to make your workflow more efficient and accurate. Synergy Research helps you to:

1. Save & organize websites, PDFs, and notes: save websites & PDFs, write down notes & text snippets, make highlights, and add tags and status.

2. Search smartly across all your content: forget about Control-F and find information instantly with full-text search, advanced search operators and synonyms.

3. Collaborate efficiently with a team: share your research content with anyone, create direct links to any text to bring anyone directly to your text selection, and make comments to collaborate easily.
