Ever wanted to share a link to the closing paragraph of a web article? Have you ever wanted to share a specific line within a webpage but didn't want to force those you send it to, to read the whole thing? LnkHppr solves this problem by letting you create links to precise sections within a webpage all with a single right click! You can even highlight what you are linking to so everyone who opens the link sees exactly what you saw!

The best part is LnkHppr works just by sharing links. We take care of everything else for you. Anyone who receives a LinkHppr link doesn't even need the extension. Seamless.
