Easy bulk actions for the new partner area of Spreadshirt. This plugin works in all countries (domains and languages) where you can find Spreadshirt.

This plugin includes many features like color changing of all selected products on the fly, csv import which can fill you forms automatically and bulk scaling of your design on all products!

Spreadshirt is your creative platform for custom t-shirts and custom clothing. You can explore our easy to use t-shirt Designer and make your own t-shirt or other personalized clothing products to spread your unique message.

Disclaimer: This is not an official plugin of Spreadshirt. There may be unexpected changes to the platform, which may cause the plugin to fail.
