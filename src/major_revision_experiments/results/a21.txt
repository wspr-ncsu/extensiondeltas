Have you ever wanted to watch a music video on Youtube and get the Lyrics to show up automatically and on time with the Music?

Say hello to Musixmatch for Youtube, the world's largest and official Lyrics catalog with +7M Lyrics in 32 languages now available as Chrome Extension.
All you need to do is to add this extension.
You can change options, colors and background (options menu - Captions)
