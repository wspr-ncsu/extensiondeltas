This extension configures your default search to searchpowerapp.com & provides enhanced results
Please read carefully: By installing Flash App (9144 400), you agree to change your Default Search to Flash App (searchpowerapp.com)

How does it work?

Simply start typing your search query in the Omni Bar, also called address bar and perform your first search through Flash App.

For more information about how Flash App works visit https://developer.chrome.com/extensions/settings_override.

Permissions:
Change your search settings to: searchpowerapp.com
tabs, webRequest, webRequestBlocking only for our domain https://*.searchpowerapp.com/*

How to remove Flash App extension?

1) Click on the three horizontal lines icon in the upper right-hand corner of your Chrome browser.
2) Go to "Settings".
3) In the menu that appears click on "Extensions".
4) From the list of installed Extensions, find the name of the extension you wish to uninstall.
5) Click on the trash can icon to the right of "Enable" (for the toolbar extension you wish to uninstall).
6) Close your browser completely and reopen it. Our extension should be removed from your Chrome browser.
