If you think that simplicity is the peak of beauty, then you will LOVE Clock Face!
Clock Face is an extension that will override your default new tab page with an aesthetic and customisable digital clock.

FEATURES:
  - No distractions and useless visual elements.
  - 25 colourful and eye pleasing visual schemes (more to come).
  - 12/24 hour clock (with or without a leading zero).
  - Toggle display for seconds and date.

And all of this completely free!

Clock Face is also available for your Android device! Search for Clock Face on Play Store!
