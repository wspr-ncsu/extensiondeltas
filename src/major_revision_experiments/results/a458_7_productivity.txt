Tools to help you secure yourself from Phishing scams within the Ethereum world.

* Detect similar Twitter handles to whitelisted cryptocurrency-based Twitter accounts and puts a warning around the tweet.
* Puts a "Verified" badge next to the tweet handle so you know the account is whitelisted.
* Blacklists/Redirects you away from known bad domains using metamask/eth-phishing-detect

Currently in BETA phase.
