Get quick access to your bookmarks, even in Fulllscreen Mode.

Once this extension is installed, use CTRL + SHIFT + U at any time to bring the modal listing your first 10 bookmarks and a searchbar, allowing you to search among your bookmarks.

Bookmarks permissions are only asked to feed the suggestions list.

Feature requests are welcome !

Controls:
- Use Arrow Up and Arrow Down to navigate between your listed bookmarks
- To open a bookmark you can either press Enter when it is selected or click directly on it
- Press Ctrl + (Enter OR Click) to open your bookmark in a new tab
