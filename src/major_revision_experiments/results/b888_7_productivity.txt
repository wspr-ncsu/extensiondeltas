"BIP Ticker" is a small Chrome extension to track dynamic and changes of BIP price.

Features:
– Auto price updates
– Price changes
– History chart
– Application with open sources (https://github.com/Raccoona-Crypto/bip-ticker-extension)

You can ask a question or leave feature issue by this link: https://github.com/Raccoona-Crypto/bip-ticker-extension/issues/new
