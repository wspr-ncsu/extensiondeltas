Remember better when you see something over and over again! LearnTab uses spaced repetition, a scientifically-proven technique to boost your memory.

How this extension work
========================

This extension helps you to remember better by showing a random saved item in your new tab.

How to use
==========

After installed, on any webpages, highlight any sentences or text, right click & choose "Add to LearnTab". You'll see a random item displayed on your new tab from the list you saved.
