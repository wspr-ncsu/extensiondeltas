Decrease the amount of time spent interacting with web forms

Automatically fills and saves the elements of a web form for you.

After installation, click on the extension icon ([A] on the top right) to enable on the current web site.

• AUTO SUBMIT – Automatically submits a web form 

• NON DYNAMIC MODE – Automatically saves and loads the content of all web form elements as soon as the form is submitted

• DYNAMIC MODE – Automatically saves and loads the content of all web form elements as soon as they are filled

• PRINT AND DELETE – Shows a resume of the application storage 

• ENABLE – Enable or disable the extension on all pages
