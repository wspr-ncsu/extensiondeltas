Simple and efficent tab cache. Dont mess your bookmarks with websites/articles/videos that you won't open more than once.

Check shortcut menu in your chrome extensions for extra usability! 
Default shortcuts:
Ctrl+Q - Cache Tab
Ctrl+Shift+Q - Open tab list

New feature! Right-click on link to cache it without opening new tab! Check it out and let me know if you like it! (mail icon) 

Extension is 100% free & ad-free. Enjoy.
