A read-it-later service for your ears. Send articles you want to read in the future to a personalized podcast feed for you to listen to on your own time.

Please create an account at ListenLater.fm to use this add-on. Once you have your account, please add your API Key to the Add-on settings in Firefox. From there, any time you want to add a new article to your personalized podcast feed, just click the icon in your toolbar.
