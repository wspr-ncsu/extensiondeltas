The next generation of MindMup - a zero-friction, collaborative, free online mindmapping tool.  MindMup 2.0 brings much better Google Drive/Google apps integration. 

- Support for typical Google Drive workflows - rename and change folders from the app, or create a map inside a folder to inherit sharing permissions so you can manage maps easier
- Show previews in Drive, so you can share and discover maps easier
- Attach files from Google Drive to map nodes
- Add images from Google Photos to map nodes
- Argument visualisation, productivity mode and several visual themes
