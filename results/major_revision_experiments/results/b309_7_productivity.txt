Simplistic, Clean and elegant; Just the way you like it.

+++FEATURES+++
-Search Youtube, Reddit, Github, IMDB and Gmail from the new tab page.
-Persistent notepad, lasting over new browsing sessions and reboots, resizeable, no max character limit,
-Time & date
-Clean, elegant design.
-Lightweight.
-Open Source
-Integration with bookmarks

+++HOW TO USE+++

After installing your new tab page will be upgraded, adding bookmarks inside a folder named "Favorites" will create an easy to use table for navigation in your bookmarks.
