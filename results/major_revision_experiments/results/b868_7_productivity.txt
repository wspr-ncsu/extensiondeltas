Itipic is a free application to collect, share image images and videos you found on the web.
Itipic act as a visual bookmarking managment tool.

With Itipic :
   
  - collect images and videos you found on the web
  - organize your collections in a private space
  - retrieve the web pages where you found your images
  - share and comment them with you wanted to

More :
  - Import in Itipic your images from your hard disk or from your smart phone
  - Send photo from your smart phone
  - Share them like web images
