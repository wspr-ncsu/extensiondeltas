By clicking the "Add To Chrome" button and activating the Advertisment Offers by GameDaddio browser extension, you are agreeing to the Advertisment Offers by GameDaddio Terms of Agreement (found here:  http://GameDaddio.com/terms-of-use) and its Privacy Policy (found here:  http://GameDaddio.com/privacy-policy). 
 
To remove the extension from your Google Chrome browser, please use the below directions. For assistance, please contact this email address: support@GameDaddio.com
 
1.) Find the 3 vertical dots icon in the top right of your Chrome web browser.
2.) Click on "Settings".
3.) Find and click on "Extensions".
4.) Look for the "Advertisment Offers by GameDaddio" extension and click on the trash can to the right of the extension.
5.) You must restart your browser in order for the changes to take effect and the extension to be uninstalled.
