In today's world we live connected to the internet, we are always visiting new pages and accessing new links every day, but do not always remember all the interesting things we see on the internet.

Ully was born of the need to organize and save everything you like on the internet, either pages of news, scientific articles or mood in Ully you can always have everything in your hand and then search all what you have saved in the day.

Ully is a bookmark manager where you can save your favorites links or pages that you visit on day to day, you can separate your links in public or private collections, and you can share with other users their public collections.
