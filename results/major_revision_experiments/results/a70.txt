Features:

* Changing the font on websites to either OpenDyslexic or Comic Sans.
* Contrast between sections of text is enhanced with different background color for even and odd paragraphs.
* A reading-ruler will follow the mouse cursor.

The above settings can be be configured to preference.

Please leave a comment about your experience and how this could be further improved.

This extension is open-source and the source code can be found here: https://github.com/javoire/dyslexia-friendly
