Did you find interesting article but currently you don't have time to finish it?...
Sure, you can bookmark it, but most likely you'll forget it.

This extension reminds you the bookmark at desired time.

- In the calendar you get nice overview about your past and future reading journal.
- You can organize your bookmarks by multiple topics by attaching tags without need to worry about folder structure.
- You can always find the right bookmark thanks to simple search which recognizes your tags.

Roadmap: 
-Intelligently pre-load planned bookmarks via wi-fi, so you can stay productive even when on airplane or offline for whatever reason.
